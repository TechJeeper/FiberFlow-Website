How FiberFlow works

Three simple steps to go from scattered skeins to a organized crafting workflow.

  1. 1

    Build your stash

    Add yarn manually or scan a label photo to auto-fill brand and weight. Use Shop Mode before buying to avoid duplicates.

  2. 2

    Import patterns

    Bring in PDFs, web links, book photos, or Ravelry patterns. Review auto-detected metadata, mark up as you craft, and hand off to a tablet via QR.

  3. 3

    Track your projects

    Create a project, link stash yarn and tools, reorder your WIP queue, export milestone reels, and recover frogged yardage back to stash.
